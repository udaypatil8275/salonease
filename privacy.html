Privacy Policy

Welcome to SalonEase! Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you interact with our salon booking and management services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Your name, phone number, email address, and profile details during signup or appointments.
  • Booking Data: Selected services, preferred time slots, and stylist preferences.
  • Payment Information: Required billing and payment details processed securely.
  • Usage Insights: Browsing patterns, device info, and interactions to enhance your experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To schedule and manage salon appointments.
  • To send appointment reminders and promotional updates (if opted in).
  • To process secure payments.
  • To improve user experience through usage analytics.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or trade your data. We may share your information with:

  • Third-party payment processors for transaction completion.
  • Trusted service providers (e.g., SMS or email services).
  • Legal authorities, if mandated by law or court order.

Your Rights

  • Access or update your personal profile data.
  • Request deletion of your account or information.
  • Opt out of marketing emails anytime.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or need assistance, please contact our team.
